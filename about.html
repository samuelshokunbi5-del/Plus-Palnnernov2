About Us

Our mission is to help students reduce stress, stay organized, and take control of their academic journey.

Our Story

Pulse Planner was built because students were drowning in deadlines with no simple way to stay on top of it all. We saw students missing assignments not because they didn't care, but because nothing helped them see the full picture. Pulse Planner gives every student — from 9th grade to senior year — the tools to plan smarter and stress less.

Our Values

📋 Organization

One place for every assignment, deadline, and study goal — no more scattered sticky notes.

🧠 Productivity

Smart scheduling that fits around your life — not the other way around.

🏆 Student Success

We measure our win by your GPA and your stress level. Both should move in the right direction.

Meet the Founder

Alex Rivera

Founder & CEO

Alex started Pulse Planner after struggling through junior year with five AP classes and zero system to track it all. After missing two major deadlines in one week, Alex decided to build the planner every overwhelmed student actually needs.
